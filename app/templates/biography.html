Who is Yewz?

A new member of the production industry, and a long-time bass lover/electronic dance music fanatic, Yewz is born. Producing a mixture of mellow, intense, and uplifting tracks, Yewz is currently a student of ill.Gates' Class of 808 in efforts to constantly stride forward in order to produce more innovative and unique music in the electronica world.

He also plans to finish up his Bachelor's of Science in Computer Software Engineering this year, and eventually provide his development skills to the world on the side.
